Body Language

Are you looking like a winner? Bold and confident. Your body language is more important than how you sound or the words you use. A lot of times, we don’t know how we look and could have small mannerisms that we are unaware of.

JVN identifies your enabling and hindering micro expressions, eye contact, frequency of smiling, hand, leg and head movement.

Based on your score and evaluation it will create a customized learning path for you which comprises learning videos, exercises and quizzes.
